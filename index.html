Visit Voss

Top three activities to do at Voss

Enjoy the beauty of the lake

The scenic town of Voss lays on the lakeshore surrounded by mountains.

Stroll through the town center

The town center known locally as Vangen is lined with shops, restaurants and cafes.

Get active

Skiing, hiking, paragliding, kayaking and cycling are all popular activities in Voss.
